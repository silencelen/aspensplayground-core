Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Welcome to Aspen's Playground ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you play our game.

Summary: We collect minimal data necessary for gameplay (player name, scores). We do not sell your data. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Player Name: The display name you choose when playing. This is stored locally on your device and submitted with leaderboard entries.
  • Leaderboard Submissions: When you complete a game and submit your score, we store your player name, score, wave reached, and zombie kills.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP Address: We collect your IP address for security purposes (rate limiting, preventing abuse, and banning malicious users).
  • Game Session Data: Temporary data about your current game session (position, health, score) which is not permanently stored.
  • Local Storage Data: Game settings, preferences, cosmetic selections, and achievements stored in your browser's localStorage.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses
  • Real names or personal identification
  • Payment information
  • Location data (beyond IP-based country)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs

2. How We Use Your Information

Data Type Purpose Legal Basis (GDPR)
Player Name Display on leaderboard, multiplayer identification Consent / Contract
Game Scores Leaderboard rankings Consent
IP Address Security, rate limiting, abuse prevention Legitimate Interest
Game Settings Personalize your experience Contract

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Data is Stored

  • Client-Side (Your Device): Player name, settings, achievements, and cosmetic preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage.
  • Server-Side: Leaderboard entries (top 10 scores) are stored on our game server.

3.2 Data Retention

  • Leaderboard Data: Retained indefinitely or until you request deletion.
  • IP Addresses: Retained for up to 30 days for security purposes, then automatically deleted.
  • Session Data: Deleted when you disconnect from the game.
  • LocalStorage: Remains until you clear your browser data or request deletion.

3.3 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Input validation and sanitization
  • Regular security reviews

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Your data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Public Leaderboard: Your chosen player name and score are visible to other players on the leaderboard.
  • Multiplayer Sessions: Your player name is visible to other players in the same game lobby.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

5.1 For All Users

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data from our servers.
  • Opt-Out: You can play without submitting to the leaderboard.

5.2 For EU/UK Users (GDPR)

  • Right to rectification (correct inaccurate data)
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

5.3 For California Users (CCPA)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (Note: We do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This game contains violence and is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not submit any personal information through the game, including player names for the leaderboard.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

This game uses browser localStorage (similar to cookies) to store:

  • Essential: Game settings required for the game to function
  • Preferences: Your chosen player name, cosmetic selections
  • Achievements: Your unlocked achievements

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings. Note that clearing localStorage will reset your game settings and achievements.

8. Third-Party Services

This game uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Three.js: 3D graphics library (no data collection)

9. International Data Transfers

If you access the game from outside the country where our servers are located, your data may be transferred internationally. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of the game after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

Data Protection Inquiries

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, to exercise your data rights, or to report a privacy concern, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@aspensplayground.com
  • Subject Line: "Privacy Request - [Your Request Type]"

We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.
